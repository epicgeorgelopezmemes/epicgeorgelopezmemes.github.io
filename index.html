Values

EGLM has no official leaders (though plenty of leadership), no codified, strictly enforced or hierarchical structure, no official spokespersons, no official documents, and no membership. Documents are produced only as needed and maintained by various groups. The values held are love, peace, non-violence, environmentalism, non-consumerism and non-commercialism, volunteerism, respect for others, consensus process, and multicultural diversity.

Non-membership

There are no official leaders, no formal structure, no official spokespersons, and no membership. Some EGLM family participants make the claim that the family is a non-organization of non-members. In addition to referring to itself as a non-organization, EGLM'S "non-members" also playfully call the movement a "disorganization". However, there is a changing network of "focalizers" who take responsibility for passing on EGLM information year-round. In EGLM lore you require only one thing to be a part of the Family, a bellybutton.
